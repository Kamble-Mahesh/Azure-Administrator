Module -1 Identity 

A. Azure Active Directory (Azure AD):-
Azure Active Directory (Azure AD) is a cloud-based identity and access management service. Which enables you to securely manage the access to
Azure services & resources for users.

Benifits of Azure AD: 

	a. Single Sign On (SSO) feature 
	b. Identity protection 
	c. Authentication
	d. Conditional Access
	e. Device Management
	f. Identity governance
	g. Identity protection
